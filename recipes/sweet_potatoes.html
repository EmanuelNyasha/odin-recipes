Roasted Sweet potatoes

Description

Here you find information on how to prepare tasty sweet potatoes

Recipe

Ingridients

  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Avocado Oil
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt and Pepper
Steps
  1. Peel or don't peel the potatoes, it's up tp you
  2. Cut the potatoes into 1/2-inch cubes
  3. Toss the sweet_potatoes in oil and seasonings
  4. Roast at 425∘F(220∘C) for 30 to 35 minutes. Halfway through, toss the cubes around so that all sides are evenly cooked

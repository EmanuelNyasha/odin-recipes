Fried Rice Recipe

Description

Here you find information on how to cook delicious fired rice to boost the happiness of your family

Recipe

Ingridients

  • 1 1/2 cups long grain rice
  • 3 large egss
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1 piece ginger
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp MSG
  • 1/3 cup frozen peas
Steps
  1. Fill a large pot halfway with water and bring to boil.
  2. Cook rice in boiling water 3 minutes, then drain with a fine-mesh strainer that fits in pot.
  3. Steam until rice is cooked through but still slightly al dente, about 15 minutes; it should have a drier feel than normal steamed rice
  4. Heat a large wok or well-seasoned cast-iron skillet over high heat. Pour in 1 tablespoon oil and swirl wok to coat. Once oil is shimmering, add eggs and cook, breaking up into small pieces, until cooked through but not browning, about 1 minute. Transfer to a plate. Wipe out wok.
  5. In same wok over high eat, swirl remaining 2 tablespoons oil. Cook garlic and ginger, tossing constantly, until fragnant and garlic begins to brown, about 30 seconds
  6. Add soy sauce, granulated sugar,salt, pepper, and MSG and cook, tossing constantly, ubtil soy is absorbed, about 1 minute. Add eggs, peas, and dark green scallion parts and cook, tossing constantly, until warmed through and incorporated, about 2 minutes more. Add butter and cook, tossing, until melted, about 30 seconds more.
